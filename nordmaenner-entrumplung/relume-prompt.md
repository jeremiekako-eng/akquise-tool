# Relume Design Prompt — Die Nordmänner
## Entrümpelung + Umzüge Kiel — Kombinierte Landingpage

---

## Project Overview

Design a single high-converting landing page for **Die Nordmänner**, a local company in **Kiel, Germany** offering both junk removal (Entrümpelung) and moving services (Umzüge). 

The page must feel bold, local, and fast — radically different from the generic teal/blue corporate look every competitor uses. This is the only company in Kiel offering both services under one roof, and the design must communicate that clearly.

---

## Brand

**Company:** Die Nordmänner
**Services:** Entrümpelung (junk removal/household clearance) + Umzüge (moving services)
**Location:** Kiel, Schleswig-Holstein, Germany
**Tone:** Direct, no-nonsense, local — like a reliable tradesman who shows up and gets it done. Not corporate, not overly friendly. Real.
**Key differentiator:** Only provider in Kiel offering both services. Fastest response time. Fixed prices.

- Two CTAs: **"Kostenloses Angebot"** (orange filled) + **"Jetzt anrufen"** (outline)

### 2. Trust Banner (full-width strip)

  - ⚡ Noch heute vor Ort
  - 💶 Festpreis garantiert

### 3. Service Switcher / Tabs

- Two tabs: **Entrümpelung** | **Umzüge**

- Headline: **"Erst räumen wir aus — dann ziehen wir ein."**

### 5. 3-Step Process

## Key Design Rules

1. **One primary CTA** — everything leads to "Kostenloses Angebot" or phone call
2. **Orange only for CTAs and accents** — not decorative
3. **Phone number always visible** on mobile (sticky header)
4. **No stock photos** — real job-site imagery, real trucks, real team
5. **Mobile-first** — most traffic from mobile search
6. **Avoid teal/blue entirely** — that's the competitor color space
7. **Combination section is mandatory** — it's the unique market differentiator
